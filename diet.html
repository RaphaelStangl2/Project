The most popular diets

Atkins diet

The Atkins diet controlls the levels of insulin in the body with a low-carbohydrate diet.

The Zone diet

The Zone diet aims for a nutritional balance of 40% carbohydrates, 30% fats, and 30% protein in each meal.

Ketogenic diet

The ketogenic diet involves reducing carbohydrate intake and upping fat intake.

Vegetarian diet

The vegetarian diet involves not eating any animal besed food, except eggs, dairy, and honey.

Vegan Diet

The vegan diet involves not eating any animal based food.

Mediterranean diet

The emphasis is on lots of plant foods and fresh fruits.
